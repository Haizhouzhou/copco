# DFM Predictability Sensitivity in Danish Natural-Reading Gaze Profiles

## Contributions

1. A prepared Danish natural-reading gaze, linguistic, LM, and label pipeline for dyslexia-labeled reader analysis.
2. A cross-fitted residualized participant sensitivity-profile method.
3. Evidence that DFM predictability sensitivity, not DFM exposure, drives strong participant-level prediction.
4. Secondary evidence that reader-group differences involve word length, DFM surprisal, and previous-boundary opacity.
