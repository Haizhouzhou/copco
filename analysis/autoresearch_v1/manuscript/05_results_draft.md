# Results Draft

The final D3 model achieved LOPO ROC-AUC 0.8947, PR-AUC 0.8641, balanced accuracy 0.8421, macro F1 0.8421, and Brier score 0.1159. Permutation p-value was 0.000999. Bootstrap intervals were ROC-AUC [0.7765, 0.9841] and PR-AUC [0.7083, 0.9728]. DFM exposure-only performed poorly, while DFM sensitivity-only and residual-gaze-only groups remained strong.
